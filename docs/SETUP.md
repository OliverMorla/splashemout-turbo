## 1. Company Snapshot

**Company name:** Splash ‘Em Out / Splash Em’ Out Wash ‘N’ Fold  
**Primary promise:** “We can take care of all your laundry needs. Big or small.”  
**Core positioning:** A locally owned, family-oriented laundry brand focused on clean, safe, fully attended laundromats and convenient laundry services for residential and commercial customers.

Splash ‘Em Out has roots in the cleaning business dating back to 2001. The owners, Joe Dan and Kelli, expanded into laundry services and opened their first laundromat in 2008. The brand now operates a network of laundromats serving Lexington, Richmond, Nicholasville, and nearby Kentucky communities.

### Brand Trust Points

- Locally owned and operated
- Family-owned feel
- Clean and safe facilities
- Fully attended laundromats
- Multiple locations across Central Kentucky
- Pickup and delivery available
- Wash-and-fold service with 24-hour turnaround
- Commercial laundry service with custom quotes
- Large-capacity machines, including up to 80 lb washers
- Customer-friendly amenities like Wi-Fi, seating, TVs, kids play area, and on-site ATMs

## 2. Website Purpose

The website should help visitors quickly answer these questions:

1. **Where is the closest Splash ‘Em Out location?**
2. **What services do they offer?**
3. **How much does laundry service cost?**
4. **How do I schedule pickup and delivery?**
5. **Can they handle business/commercial laundry?**
6. **Are the laundromats clean, safe, and convenient?**
7. **How do I contact or request a quote?**

The site should serve both immediate local-intent users and recurring service customers.

People who need self-service laundry, drop-off wash-and-fold, pickup/delivery laundry, comforter washing, blanket cleaning, or dry cleaning pickup/delivery.

**Likely needs:**

**Likely needs:**

### Students

University of Kentucky, Eastern Kentucky University, and nearby college students.

**Likely needs:**

- Affordable laundry
- Nearby locations

### Commercial Customers

Hotels, Airbnb/VRBO/vacation rental hosts, restaurants, bars, spas, salons, medical offices, clinics, veterinary offices, universities, equine businesses, gyms, and other companies with recurring laundry needs.

**Likely needs:**

- Custom pricing

Customers drop off laundry, and Splash ‘Em Out washes, dries, and folds it.

## 16. FAQ Content to Include

### Laundromat

**Can I pay by credit card?**  
Yes. Washers and dryers accept both coins and credit cards.

**Do you have large machines?**  
Yes. Splash ‘Em Out offers large-capacity machines, including washers large enough for king-size comforters.

**Do you have a loyalty program?**  
Yes. The site mentions that customers can earn rewards through the laundry card program.

### Wash & Fold

**What is the turnaround time?**  
Wash-and-fold turnaround is 24 hours.

**Is there a minimum order?**  
Yes. Drop-off wash-and-fold has a $15 minimum.

**Do you hang items?**  
Yes. Items can be hung on request. There is no charge if customers provide hangers; if Splash ‘Em Out provides hangers, it is $0.50 per hanger.

### Pickup & Delivery

**What is the turnaround time?**  
Pickup and delivery turnaround is typically 24–48 hours.

**Is there a minimum order?**  
Yes. Pickup and delivery has a $30 minimum.

**Do I need to be home?**  
No. Customers can specify where to leave laundry for pickup.

**Do recurring customers sign a contract?**  
No. The FAQ states there are no contracts.
