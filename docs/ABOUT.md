# Splash 'Em Out Company Summary

## What Splash 'Em Out Is

Splash 'Em Out is a Central Kentucky laundry service brand with clean, fully attended laundromats plus wash-and-fold, pickup and delivery, dry cleaning pickup and delivery, and commercial laundry service.

The business serves residential customers, busy families, students, and recurring commercial accounts across Lexington, Richmond, Nicholasville, and nearby service areas.

## Purpose

The website should turn local laundry intent into action quickly:

- Help people find the closest laundromat.
- Make wash-and-fold and pickup pricing easy to understand.
- Move pickup and delivery customers into online scheduling.
- Give commercial buyers a clear path to request a bid.
- Reinforce that the facilities are clean, safe, attended, and practical.

The site should feel like a useful local service platform, not a lifestyle blog or a generic small-business brochure.

## Core Audience

Primary audience:

- People searching for a clean laundromat near them.
- Customers who want drop-off wash-and-fold instead of doing laundry at home.
- Busy households with weekly loads, bedding, uniforms, towels, and kids' clothes.
- Students near University of Kentucky and Eastern Kentucky University.
- Customers who want laundry picked up and returned ready to put away.

Commercial audience:

- Airbnb, VRBO, and short-term rental hosts.
- Restaurants, bars, spas, salons, gyms, clinics, and veterinary offices.
- Hotels, universities, equine businesses, and teams with recurring towels, linens, uniforms, blankets, or specialty laundry.
- Operators who need dependable pickup, fast turnaround, and custom pricing.

## Product Goals

The site should:

- Make the nearest-location path obvious above the fold.
- Make "pickup and delivery" and "drop-off wash-and-fold" clearly different.
- Show prices before visitors have to hunt for them.
- Promote trust with real facility details, attended locations, hours, phone numbers, and service areas.
- Support local SEO with useful service and location pages.
- Generate qualified commercial quote requests.
- Keep mobile CTAs visible for schedule, call, and directions.

The site should not:

- Hide pricing behind a form.
- Make commercial customers search for the bid path.
- Use vague national-laundry-service language.
- Rely on generic stock photos instead of local proof.
- Create thin duplicate city pages.
- Overcomplicate navigation with every service variation in the top level.

## Service Model

Splash 'Em Out is built around five customer jobs:

1. Self-service laundry: clean, attended laundromats with large-capacity machines and useful amenities.
2. Drop-off wash-and-fold: next-day laundry service starting at $1.69/lb with a $15 minimum.
3. Pickup and delivery: online scheduling, 24-48 hour turnaround, recurring or as-needed service, and a $30 minimum.
4. Commercial laundry: custom recurring, next-day, and emergency service for businesses.
5. Dry cleaning pickup and delivery: convenient garment-care pickup and return for Lexington and nearby areas.

## Pricing Direction

Pricing should be visible and plain:

- Drop-off wash-and-fold: $1.69/lb, $15 minimum.
- Pickup and delivery recurring: $2.09/lb, $30 minimum.
- Pickup and delivery as needed: $2.39/lb, $30 minimum.
- Individually priced bulky items should appear on the pricing page.
- Commercial pricing should stay quote-based with a detailed request form.

Promotions:

- Free laundry bag for first pickup and delivery order.
- 10% off wash-and-fold for military customers and first responders.

## Trust And Operations

The site should reinforce operational trust through specifics:

- Locally owned and operated.
- Family-oriented brand history.
- Fully attended laundromats.
- Clean and safe facilities.
- Multiple Central Kentucky locations.
- Large machines up to 80 lb washers.
- Wi-Fi, seating, TVs, kids play area, ATMs, and customer-friendly amenities where available.
- Clear phone numbers, hours, addresses, and service-area coverage.

Hours differ in some source material. Verify final location hours with the client before launch.

## Success Definition

Splash 'Em Out succeeds online if visitors can quickly say:

- "I found the closest location."
- "I know what service I need."
- "I understand the price."
- "I can schedule pickup now."
- "This looks clean, local, and dependable."
- "My business can request a quote without extra back-and-forth."
