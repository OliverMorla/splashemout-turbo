Brief:

- Page kind: redesign and rebuild for a local service marketing homepage.
- Product: Splash 'Em Out, a Central Kentucky laundry service with clean fully attended laundromats, drop-off wash-and-fold, pickup and delivery, dry cleaning pickup and delivery, and commercial laundry.
- Audience: local people who need laundry handled now. This includes near-me laundromat searchers, busy families, students, pickup and delivery customers, and business operators comparing dependable laundry vendors.
- Vibe words: clean, useful, bright, locally trusted.
- References: Tailwind neutral page structure, high-clarity service locator, pricing-forward local service pages, modern map and route UI, and real facility/process photography. Core colors: Clean Water blue `#0EA5E9`, Deep Water `#075985`, Fresh Mint `#14B8A6`, Citrus promo `#F59E0B`, plus neutral text and surfaces. Use a memorable "Load Path" route motif to show laundry moving from home or business to clean return.
- Avoid: generic SaaS hero, beige wellness palette, purple AI gradient, dark moody laundromat visuals, stock family laundry photos, bubble overload, fluffy brand storytelling before CTAs, hidden pricing, and any design that makes commercial buyers hunt for the bid form.
