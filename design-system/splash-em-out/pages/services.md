# Services Page Override

Use this file for `/services` and service detail pages.

## Goal

Help visitors choose the correct service quickly and understand the next action for each service.

## Service Differentiation

Each service block must answer:

- Who is this for?
- What does Splash 'Em Out do?
- What does it cost or how is it quoted?
- What is the turnaround?
- What should the user do next?

## Required Service Paths

- Self-service laundry: Find a Location.
- Drop-off wash-and-fold: Find a Location or View Pricing.
- Pickup and delivery: Schedule Pickup.
- Commercial laundry: Request a Bid.
- Dry cleaning pickup and delivery: Schedule Pickup or See Details.

## Layout

Avoid identical icon-card grids as the only structure. Use service splits, comparison rows, process steps, and pricing callouts where they reduce ambiguity.
