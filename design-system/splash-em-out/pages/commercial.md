# Commercial Page Override

Use this file for `/commercial` and `/commercial/request-a-bid`.

## Goal

Make Splash 'Em Out credible for recurring business laundry and collect enough quote information for useful follow-up.

## Page Priorities

1. State commercial laundry capability plainly.
2. Name likely industries: short-term rentals, restaurants, spas, salons, gyms, clinics, veterinary offices, hotels, universities, equine businesses, and teams.
3. Explain pickup, turnaround, volume, frequency, and custom pricing.
4. Provide Request a Bid as the primary action.

## Quote Form Fields

Group fields by:

- Business details.
- Contact information.
- Pickup/service address.
- Industry.
- Laundry types.
- Estimated volume.
- Pickup frequency.
- Turnaround needs.
- Notes or special handling.

## Copy And Claims

Stay operational and specific. Do not promise regulated medical laundry, guaranteed emergency service, or exact commercial pricing unless verified.
