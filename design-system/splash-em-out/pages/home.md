# Home Page Override

Use this file for `/`.

## Goal

The homepage must turn broad local laundry intent into one of three actions: schedule pickup, find a location, or understand pricing. It should also make commercial laundry visible without making residential users work harder.

## Above The Fold

Must include:

- Clear service promise.
- Central Kentucky service context.
- Primary CTA: Schedule Pickup.
- Secondary CTA: Find a Location.
- A visible proof cue: attended laundromats, pricing, local service area, or real pickup/drop-off imagery.

Avoid abstract brand statements that delay service clarity.

## Recommended Flow

1. Hero with schedule and location CTAs.
2. Location finder or location preview.
3. Service choice section: self-service, wash-and-fold, pickup and delivery, dry cleaning pickup, commercial.
4. Pricing snapshot with minimums.
5. Trust proof: attended locations, clean facilities, local ownership, machine capacity, service areas.
6. Commercial strip with Request a Bid.
7. Final CTA with Schedule Pickup and Find a Location.

## Interaction Rules

- Keep mobile sticky actions for Schedule and Call if they do not hide content.
- Service cards should link to service pages and clarify the customer job.
- Location preview should prioritize address, directions, and phone over decorative copy.
